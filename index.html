About Thierry

Thierry Ascarez is the lucky father of three boys. Born and raised in Belgium, Thierry is an author and entrepreneurial adventurer who relocated to San Francisco, California in September of 2012 to start a tech subsidiary specialized in digital audio. While living the fast pace and very exhilarating life as a startup revolutionary in Silicon Valley, Thierry, with the help of his beautiful wife Alexandra, worked intensively to make his family just as much of a priority and success as his business.

Though at times things were difficult, Thierry used his savvy entrepreneurial spirit to create a healthy balance between work and family to become a successful dadpreneur. He wants to share that journey and all the tips and tricks he learned along the way to help other dads and expecting fathers along the way.
